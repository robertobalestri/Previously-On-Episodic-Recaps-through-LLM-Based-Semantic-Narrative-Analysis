### Key Episode Events:
- Meredith wakes up after a one-night stand with Derek.
- Meredith starts her first day as an intern at Seattle Grace Hospital.
- Interns meet their resident, Miranda Bailey, and begin their duties.
- Meredith is assigned to patient Katie Bryce, who is having seizures.
- George is chosen for an appendectomy but struggles.
- Meredith discovers Derek is her boss.
- Meredith and Cristina work together to diagnose Katie's condition.
- Meredith assists in Katie's surgery.
- Meredith decides not to sell her mother's house and reveals Ellis Grey's Alzheimer's.

### Narrative Arcs:

**Description:** Meredith and Derek's relationship begins with a one-night stand and evolves into a complicated romance, affecting their professional lives and friendships.
