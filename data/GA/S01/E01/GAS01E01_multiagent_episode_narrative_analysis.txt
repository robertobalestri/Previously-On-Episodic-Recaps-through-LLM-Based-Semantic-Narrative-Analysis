### 1. **Key Episode Events**:
- Meredith wakes up on her couch after a one-night stand with Derek.
- She prepares for her first day at Seattle Grace Hospital, feeling embarrassed about the previous night.
- Meredith meets her fellow interns, including Cristina, George, and Izzie, and learns about their resident, Bailey.
- The interns face their first medical case involving Katie Bryce, who is having seizures.
- Meredith discovers Derek is her boss, complicating their relationship.
- George struggles during a procedure, leading to a nickname that worries him.
- Meredith and Cristina work together to diagnose Katie's condition, leading to a successful surgery.
- The episode concludes with Meredith's emotional connection to her mother, Ellis Grey, who has Alzheimer's disease.
