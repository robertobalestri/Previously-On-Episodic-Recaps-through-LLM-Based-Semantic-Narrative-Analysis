1. **Key Episode Events**:
   - Meredith wakes up after a one-night stand with Derek.
   - Meredith starts her first day as an intern at Seattle Grace Hospital.
   - Introduction of fellow interns Cristina, George, and Izzie.
   - Meredith and Cristina discover Katie Bryce's condition.
   - Meredith assists Derek in surgery.
   - Meredith decides not to sell her mother's house.
   - Revelation of Ellis Grey's Alzheimer's.

2. **Narrative Arcs**:
   - **Meredith and Derek's Relationship**: Begins with a one-night stand, complicated by professional dynamics.
   - **Meredith's Personal Struggles**: Balancing her career and her mother's Alzheimer's.
   - **Intern Dynamics**: Formation of friendships and rivalries among the interns.
   - **Cristina's Ambition**: Her competitive nature and initial conflict with Meredith.
   - **George's Challenges**: Professional embarrassment and personal growth.

3. **Themes and Motifs**:
   - **Professional and Personal Boundaries**: Meredith's relationship with Derek and her role as an intern.
   - **Friendship and Rivalry**: Developing dynamics among the interns.
   - **Responsibility and Growth**: Meredith's decision-making and surgical involvement.
   - **Secrets and Revelations**: Ellis Grey's condition and Meredith's background.
   - **Love and Betrayal**: Initial romantic tension between Meredith and Derek.
